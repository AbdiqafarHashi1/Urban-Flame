OUR STORY

Bringing Bold Flavors to Eastleigh

Urban Flame was born from our passion for authentic, bold flavors and the vibrant energy of Nairobi's Eastleigh neighborhood. We believe everyone deserves a fresh, customizable meal that excites the taste buds.

Our ingredients are sourced locally whenever possible, and we prepare everything fresh daily. No freezers, no shortcuts - just real food with real flavor.

Our Mission

To ignite passion for bold flavors through fresh, customizable cuisine.
